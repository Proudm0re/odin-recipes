Enchiladas

We'll show you how to make this luxurious mexican dish right from the comfort of your own home!
  • 500g of Chicken Breast
  • 200g of Cheese
  • 4x Tortilla wraps
  • Salsa
  • 250g of Refried Beans
  • 500g of Tomato Puree
  • 100g of Mexican Spices

Steps

  1. Add 200ml of water with the tomato puree to a bowl and mix together with the spices with a spoon
  2. Cut the chicken into small chunks and cook with a little oil on a frying pan
  3. Add half of the sauce you made earlier to the pan, stirring into the chicken
  4. Grate the cheese and add half of it into the pan, stir thouroughly
  5. Microwave the tortillas for 30 seconds, splitting them up and warming them nicely
  6. Fill the wraps with an even amount of chicken and sauce before wrapping them up and placing in an oven-proof dish
  7. Spread the remainer of the sauce over the top of the enchiladas, covering them completely
  8. Add the other half of the cheese over the top
  9. Set a timer for 20 minutes and oven at 200C
  10. While they cook in the oven, heat the refried beans in a pan
  11. When ready, dish up the beans and place 2 enchiladas over the top of the beans before spreading salsa along the tortillas
  12. Viola! Your very own mexican enchiladas are ready to be eaten!
