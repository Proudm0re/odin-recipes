Dark n' Stormy Cocktail Recipe

We'll show you how to make a sophisticated dark n' stormy cocktail!

What you'll need:
  • Ice
  • 4oz of your favourite Ginger beer
  • 2oz of your favourite Dark Rum
  • Lime
What to do:
  1. Fill a glass (Highball is best) with large chunks of ice
  2. Fill almost completely with ginger beer
  3. Top the remainer of the glass with dark rum
  4. Stir slightly and gently before garnishing with a wedge of lime
