Cheesecake

We'll show you how to make a beautifully delicious and creamy cheesecake!

What you'll need:
  • 250g of Digestive Biscuits
  • 100g of Butter (melting)
  • 1x Vanilla pod
  • 600g Full fat soft Cheese
  • 100g of Icing Sugar
  • 284ml Pot of double cream
What to do:
  1. Make the base by buttering and lining a tin, put the digestives in a bag and crush them thouroughly. Transfer them into a bowl and pour over the melted butter
  2. Slice the vanilla pod in half lengthways, leaving the tip intact, so that the two halves are still joined. Holding onto the tip of the pod, scrape out the seeds using the back of a kitchen knife.
  3. Pour the double cream into a bowl and whisk with an electric mixer until itâ€™s just starting to thicken to soft peaks. Place the soft cheese, icing sugar and the vanilla seeds in a separate bowl, then beat for 2 mins with an electric mixer until smooth/li>
  4. Tip in the double cream and fold it into the soft cheese mix. Smooth the top of the cheesecake down with the back of a dessert spoon or spatula. Leave to set in the fridge overnight. Bring the cheesecake to room temperature about 30 mins before serving!
